Xue Min Ng

Educational Psychologist | Founder of Growing Minds

Xue Min is an Educational Psychologist with experience supporting children across early childhood and school-age settings in both Malaysia and New Zealand.

She is passionate about helping families understand the skills behind learning and creating personalised pathways that empower children to grow with confidence.

Our Mission

To help families understand how their children learn and to nurture the skills behind confident, independent and lifelong learning.

Our Vision

To become Malaysia's leading Child Development & Learning Studio, partnering with families and schools to help every child flourish through personalised, evidence-informed support.

Our Philosophy

Every child has a unique way of learning. When we understand how children think, communicate, regulate emotions and approach challenges, we can better support their growth.

Rather than focusing solely on behaviour or academic performance, Growing Minds helps children build the underlying skills that influence learning, confidence, relationships and independence.
