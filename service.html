📋 Learning Profile

Discover Your Child's Unique Way of Learning

Every child learns differently. A Learning Profile provides a comprehensive understanding of how your child thinks, learns, communicates and approaches everyday learning.

A Learning Profile may explore:

  • Learning strengths
  • Communication
  • Language development
  • Executive functioning
  • Attention
  • Emotional regulation
  • Social development
  • Learning behaviours
  • Independence
  • School participation

The journey includes:

  • Initial consultation
  • Parent discussion
  • Standardised assessments (when appropriate)
  • Observations (when appropriate)
  • Personalised Learning Profile Report
  • Feedback session
  • Practical recommendations for home and school
