Why Growing Minds

Every child learns differently

Growing Minds is a Child Development & Learning Studio founded by an Educational Psychologist. We help families understand how their child learns—not just what they can do.

By identifying each child's unique learning profile, we create personalised pathways that strengthen the emotional, social, thinking and learning skills that support lifelong success.

Growing Minds welcomes children of all learning styles and developmental profiles—from those seeking enrichment and school readiness to those who would benefit from additional learning support.

Our goal is simple:

Understand first. Support intentionally. Help every child thrive.

The Growing Minds Framework™

Five Pillars Behind Every Child's Growth

Everything we do is built upon five developmental pillars that support the whole child, not just academic performance.

🌱

Self-Awareness

Helping children understand themselves as learners.

Emotional awareness Confidence Self-advocacy Recognising strengths Understanding challenges Growth mindset
🌱

Self-Management

Developing emotional regulation and self-control.

Emotional regulation Attention Impulse control Flexibility Coping strategies Behaviour regulation Transition skills
🌱

Social Connection

Building communication and relationships.

Communication Friendship Perspective taking Collaboration Social thinking Conflict resolution Cooperative play
🌱

Thinking Skills

Strengthening the cognitive skills behind learning.

Executive functioning Working memory Planning Organisation Flexible thinking Problem solving Verbal reasoning Self-monitoring

Learning Independence

Helping children become confident, independent learners.

Learning behaviours Following instructions Classroom participation Task completion Organisation Persistence Independence Confidence
